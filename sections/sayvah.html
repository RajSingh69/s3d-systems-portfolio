Current Project

SayVah — UK’s first seva app.

SayVah is my current flagship project: a mobile app designed to connect Sikh sevadaars with people in the wider non-Sikh community who need help.

Service through software.

The idea is simple: someone needs support, a request is created, a sevadaar responds, and real-world help happens. SayVah combines community values, safety-focused flows and modern app technology.
