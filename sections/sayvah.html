Current flagship project
SayVah

Real-time community support, built around trust.

SayVah is a seva-focused mobile app designed to connect people who need help with local sevadaars who are ready to support. It brings request creation, helper matching, safety checks, trust profiles, reporting, admin oversight, and future community tools into one platform.

Product story

From request to real-world support.

The app is structured around simple flows that keep the experience practical, safe, and easy to understand.

01

Create

Someone creates a seva request using templates, descriptions, location details, and service type.

02

Browse

Sevadaars can view open requests, filter by type, and understand what help is needed.

03

Accept

A helper accepts the request, starts the support flow, and can communicate safely.

04

Protect

Trust profiles, reports, admin tools, and verification features help keep the platform safer.

Why it matters

Helping should be easier to start.

SayVah is not just a request board. It is designed as a trust-based support system: requesters get a clearer way to ask, helpers get a safer way to respond, and admins get oversight to protect the community.
